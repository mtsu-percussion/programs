  • Heart of the Sunrise
    Yes
    (formed 1968)
    Representing the ideas and struggles of the past we have yet to face, Heart of the Sunrise was a piece I’ve been wanting to transcribe and play for years. This gives me the perfect opportunity. The best time to plant a shade tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is right now.
  • Folds
    Casey Cangelosi
    (b. 1982)
    Folds represents the ideas and struggles of the present. This piece immediately hooked me. I knew it would be a lot of hard work, but here we are. Always run toward and through the storm when it hits, never away.
